## 1. Who we are

This Policy explains how Infinity collects, uses, and shares information through infinitysmartliving.com (the "Site") and in the course of providing our services. It applies to the Site and to information you give us when you request a consultation or place an order.

## 2. Information we collect

- **Information you provide:** name, email, phone number, home address or service location, details about your home, and anything you include in a form, call, or message.
- **Information collected automatically:** device and browser data, IP address, pages viewed, and similar analytics data collected through cookies and similar technologies.
- **Advertising and measurement data:** identifiers and event data collected through advertising and analytics tools, including the Meta pixel and Google tools, used to measure and improve our campaigns.

## 3. How we use information

We use information to: respond to your consultation request and prepare your custom floor plan; price, sell, schedule, and fulfill your order; coordinate the licensed local contractor who performs your installation; communicate with you about your consultation and order; operate, secure, and improve the Site; measure and improve our advertising; and comply with law.

## 4. How we share information

We share information only as needed to run the business:

- **With the licensed local contractor** engaged to perform your installation, so they can schedule and complete the work. These contractors act as our subcontractors and service providers for that purpose.
- **With service providers** that support us (for example our CRM and booking platform, hosting, communications, and payment processing), who may use the information only to provide services to us.
- **With advertising and analytics partners** as described in Section 2, subject to their own policies.
- **When required by law**, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of others.
- **In a business transfer**, if the company is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal information for money.
