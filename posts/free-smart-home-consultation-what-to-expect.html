What a Smart Home Consultation Actually Is

Our consultation is a friendly, no-pressure video or phone call. Think of it as a conversation, not a sales pitch. During the call we cover what you are hoping to add, whether that is smart lighting, cameras and security, a video doorbell, smart locks, thermostats, automated blinds, or a full setup that ties everything together. You do not need to know any of the technical terms. That is our job.

How Long It Takes

Most consultations run about 30 to 45 minutes. If you have a larger home or a longer wish list, we may go a little past that, and that is fine. We never rush you off the call. The goal is for you to walk away with a clear picture of your options, not to push you into a decision on the spot.

What to Have Ready

You do not need to prepare much, but a little prep helps us give you better recommendations. Here is what is useful to have on hand:

  • A floor plan or rough sketch. Even a hand-drawn layout of your rooms helps us picture device placement. A builder's floor plan is great if you have one, but it is not required.
  • A wish list. Jot down what is bugging you or what you wish your home could do. For example: "I want to see who is at the front door from my phone," or "I want the lights to turn off when we leave."
  • Your internet details. Knowing your Wi-Fi setup and whether you have dead spots helps us plan for reliable performance.
  • Any existing devices. List smart products you already own, like an Alexa speaker or a smart thermostat, so we can build around them.
  • A rough budget range. Sharing a ballpark figure lets us suggest options that fit, with no awkward surprises later.
  • Questions. Write down anything you are curious or unsure about so nothing gets forgotten.
